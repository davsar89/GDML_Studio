## Important Notes

1. **G4_Galactic** is Geant4's "vacuum" material. Density ~1e-25 g/cm3, T=2.73K, P=3e-18 Pa.
2. **G4_GRAPHITE** (2.21 g/cm3) is distinct from **G4_C** (2.0 g/cm3) — same element, different density.
3. **G4_GRAPHITE_POROUS** (1.7 g/cm3) is a third carbon variant.
4. **DNA bonded materials** (G4_DNA_*) have placeholder density 1.0 g/cm3 and account for atoms lost during chemical bonding.
5. **Gas elements**: H, He, N, O, F, Ne, Cl, Ar, Br, Kr, Xe, Rn are in gas state.
6. **Liquid elements**: Hg is solid in the database (despite being liquid at room temp).
7. Materials prefixed with `l` are liquid forms: G4_lH2, G4_lN2, G4_lO2, G4_lAr, G4_lBr, G4_lKr, G4_lXe, G4_lPROPANE.
